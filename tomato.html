Tomato

Solanum lycopersicum — hungry, thirsty, productive summer crop.

Full sunRegular waterFeed weeklySupport needed

Quick Care

WaterConsistent moisture
FeedingWeekly tomato feed once flowering
AspectWarm full sun
PruningRemove side shoots for cordon types

Notes

Avoid inconsistent watering, which can contribute to fruit splitting and blossom end rot. Use a cane, string, or frame for support.

Seasonal Actions

Spring: Pot on seedlings and protect from cold.

Early summer: Plant out after frost risk.

Summer: Feed, water and tie in regularly.

Late summer: Remove lower leaves and ripen fruit.
