Care guide for this pot

Light: Bright light to full sun, depending on the exact plants.

Water: Let the pot dry more than a normal bedding pot. Succulents hate wet feet.

Feeding: Light feeding only. Too much feed can make growth soft.

Deadheading / pruning: Remove spent flower stems and tidy dead material.

Overwintering: Keep drainage sharp. Protect tender plants and hidden bulbs/corms where needed.
