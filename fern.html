Fern

Shade-tolerant foliage plant — ideal for damp, sheltered corners.

Part shadeMoist soilLow feedRemove old fronds

Quick Care

WaterKeep evenly moist
FeedingLight feed in growing season
AspectPart shade to shade
PruningCut old fronds at base

Notes

Remove brown, tired fronds once new growth is established. Avoid drying out completely, especially in pots.

Seasonal Actions

Spring: Watch for new croziers and tidy old growth.

Summer: Keep moist during dry spells.

Autumn: Leave some natural cover if outdoors.

Winter: Avoid exposed drying winds.
