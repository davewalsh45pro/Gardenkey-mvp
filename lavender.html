Lavender

Lavandula angustifolia — scented, sun-loving, pollinator friendly.

Full sunLow waterFree-draining soilBee friendly

Quick Care

WaterLightly once soil is dry
FeedingMinimal feeding needed
AspectFull sun, sheltered spot
PruningTrim after flowering

Notes

Do not overwater. Lavender dislikes sitting in wet compost. Add grit or perlite if the potting mix is too rich or heavy.

Seasonal Actions

Spring: Check drainage and remove dead growth.

Summer: Enjoy flowers and pollinators.

Late summer: Lightly prune to keep shape.

Winter: Keep away from waterlogged conditions.
