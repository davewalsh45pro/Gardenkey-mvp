# GardenKey V23 review

V23 focuses on making the existing product easier to use on a phone rather than expanding the feature count.

## Key validation journey
1. Open a general plant guide.
2. Add it as a specific My Plant record or directly to a saved pot.
3. Save without scrolling to the bottom of a long page.
4. See cultivar-style names matched to the correct general species.
5. Reopen pots and personal plants by their human-readable names.

## Recommended access model
Keep general horticultural information browseable during the pilot. For a commercial version, attach paid value to physical keys and account features rather than locking one information page to one key. A practical model is a small free allowance of personal records, with additional capacity activated by a GardenKey pack or subscription.
